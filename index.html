About Hon. Eng. Kyobe Luke Inyensiko

Hon. Eng. Kyobe Luke Inyensiko is the esteemed Member of Parliament representing Luuka North Constituency in the Parliament of the Republic of Uganda, serving since 2021. An accomplished engineer by profession, Hon. Kyobe has combined his technical expertise with a passion for leadership, making him a distinguished figure both in his community and in the national arena.

Hon. Kyobe's decision to stand for leadership was driven by a deep desire to access and utilize resources to bring transformative change to Luuka North and Luuka District as a whole. His vision is centered on development, empowerment, and the betterment of his peopleâ€™s lives.
