Achievements

During the last term, we achieved significant milestones across various sectors including Health, Education, Social Economic Development, Community Support, and Monitoring. Below is a chronological summary of our achievements.

Health

August 2015

Donated an Ambulance

In 2015, we donated an ambulance that saved many lives, especially for women...

In 2015, we donated an ambulance that played a crucial role in saving many lives, particularly benefiting women. This ambulance was instrumental in providing timely medical assistance in remote areas, improving health outcomes significantly. It is a testament to our commitment to enhancing healthcare accessibility and emergency response capabilities in our community...

August 2024

Upgraded local clinics

Renovated the Operating Theatre

Renovated the operating theatre at Kiyunga Health Center IV, improving surgical capabilities...

We undertook a major renovation of the operating theatre at Kiyunga Health Center IV, which serves as the District's primary health unit. This renovation included upgrading equipment, improving sanitation standards, and enhancing the overall functionality of the facility. These improvements have greatly increased the quality of surgical care provided to the local population...

August 2024

Shade for Maternity Ward

Installed shade structures for patients and caregivers in the maternity ward...

We installed shade structures to provide relief to patients and caregivers in the maternity ward during hot days. This initiative aimed to enhance comfort and protect individuals from excessive heat while waiting or receiving care. The added shade has significantly improved the overall experience for those visiting the maternity ward...

August 2024

Doctor at Kiyunga Health Center IV

Collaborated with the Ministry of Public Services to ensure a fully settled doctor at Kiyunga Health Center IV...

We worked closely with the Ministry of Public Services to ensure the permanent placement of a doctor at Kiyunga Health Center IV. This collaboration aimed to address the critical shortage of medical professionals and ensure continuous, high-quality healthcare services for the community. The presence of a fully settled doctor has greatly improved patient care and service availability...

August 2024

Blood Supply

Collaborated with the government and partners to maintain a steady blood supply at the facility...

We partnered with the government and various organizations to ensure a consistent supply of blood at our health facility. This initiative was crucial in addressing emergencies and providing essential blood transfusions for patients in need. Regular blood donations and storage practices have enhanced our ability to manage critical situations effectively...

Mattresses, Beds, and Bed Sheets

We made significant donations to Kiyunga Health Centre IV, including mattresses, beds, and bed sheets. These essential items were provided to improve patient comfort and hygiene within the facility. Our contribution has helped to enhance the quality of care and ensure that patients have a more comfortable and supportive environment during their stay...
